How it happened

2014

Started learning networking

Began hands-on work with last-mile fiber and copper infrastructure — provisioning, troubleshooting, and field deployment.

2016

Installed the first fiber network

Moved from theory to the field — pulling fiber, splicing, and getting the first subscribers online.

2019

Managed GPON / EPON deployments

Deep, day-to-day work with BDCOM and Huawei OLT platforms — LLID provisioning, signal diagnostics, ONU configuration at scale.

2022
